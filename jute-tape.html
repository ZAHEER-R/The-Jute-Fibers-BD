Jute / Burlap Tape - Product From The Jute Fibers BD

Jute tape/webbing is a strong, narrow, closely woven fabric used prominently in cable industry as binding for the carpets. This product is also used as a seat belt and in upholstery.

Jute tape is usually natural, striped, colored can be manufactured any width as per buyers requirements.

Size : Width: 10 cm, 15 cm, 20 cm, 30 cm, 40 cm, 60 cm, 100 cm, 120 cm
Length : 16 mtr, 16.5 mtr, 17 mtr, 18 mtr, 20 mtr, 50 mtr Both side over lock sewn. Ex fabrics CBC
Construction : (7×7) (9×8)
Packing : Paper box
Uses : These tapes are generally using for wrapping the tree & tree roots to protect themselves from Cold, Snow & insects etc.
